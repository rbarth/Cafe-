The Chuckwagon Café is brimming with hearty meals and delicious treats. Breakfast and lunch are served all day, so you can have a burger at nine in the morning or an omelet at one in the afternoon. Or both! Sit back and stay awhile, the wi-fi is free and the company is great!

All our food is homemade, fresh, and served to order. And don't forget the sides and beverages!


NOTICE: We will cook your eggs and meat to order, although consuming raw or undercooked eggs and meat may increase your risk of foodborne illness.
