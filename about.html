About

Shirley’s Chuckwagon Café has been providing friendly service and affordable prices for 13 years. Now under new ownership, we hope to be able to continue this tradition for many years to come. We serve breakfast all day and have an expanded menu of hearty, made-from-scratch comfort foods. We also have a children’s menu for 12 and under.

Mission Statement

Our mission is to ensure that each guest receives prompt, professional, friendly and courteous service, which we express by greeting and thanking each guest for the opportunity to serve. We strive to maintain a clean, comfortable, and well-maintained establishment for our guests and staff and to ensure that they are treated with the respect and dignity that they deserve. We provide a fair priced, nutritional, well-prepared menu using only quality, fresh ingredients. By maintaining these objectives, we shall be assured of a fair profit that will allow us to contribute to the community we serve.

Vision Statement

As part of our mission to provide a friendly and courteous service to our guests, our first priority in future implementations is to become handicap accessible and fully comply with disabilities codes. Additionally, we would like to be able to extend our dining area by renovating and utilizing extra storage space. This would allow everyone with restrictions to be able to enjoy the friendly atmosphere and great food that we provide. This project would include installing ramps outside of the building, and adding two new bathrooms. Our goal is to become fully handicap accessible by the end of 2015.

Additionally, we own a small building located on Main Street that is connected to our storage and future handicap access entrance. We currently offer a limited number of baked goods, but despite the high demand, we are limited within our kitchen area. In order to create an even better experience for our guests, we would like to renovate this small building into a Bakery/Ice Cream shop, offering soft-serve ice cream along with possible curbside window service. Our goal for this project would be completion by the end of 2016.
