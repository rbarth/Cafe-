Great Home Style Cookin’

Shirley’s Chuckwagon Café offers a friendly atmosphere and the best homemade home-style cooking in Nashville, MI. Breakfast is served all day, every day but if you are hankering for a good burger, pizza, or anything else lunch/ dinner related, then fear not, for the Chuckwagon serves that too!

Recieve 10% off meals by staying at Janette's Bunkhouse just behind the Chuckwagon!
